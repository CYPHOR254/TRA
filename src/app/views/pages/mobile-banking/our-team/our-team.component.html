Our Team

At Tourism Regulatory Authority (TRA), our Auditors & Assessors Team stands as a cornerstone of excellence in the tourism sector. With a group of highly skilled professionals who are passionate about upholding the highest standards, we are committed to delivering exceptional service and adhering to sustainable tourism practices. Our team conducts comprehensive assessments and audits to ensure alignment with the regulations and guidelines set forth by the national tourism strategy. By collaborating closely with stakeholders and the relevant Ministry, we continuously strive to improve our services and contribute to the growth of the tourism industry in Kenya. Transparent, ethical, and dedicated to responsible tourism, our Auditors & Assessors Team ensures unforgettable experiences while preserving the beauty and integrity of our destinations.
